> Clea is designed for the hands instant disinfection in the public places.

It emotionally invites you to use it by putting your hands between the two stripes, and after the disinfectant liquid spraying, the Clea luminescence becomes brighter letting you know, that the cleaning procedure is over. At the same time Clea does not only disinfect the hands, it also refreshes them.

Its futuristic design increases the interest to this device and attracts more and more people to care for their own hygiene.
